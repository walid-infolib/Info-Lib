Stamp Tax

Stamp tax on invoices

To Configure:


Nothing to configure, just install the module.

Check the stamp tax in the accounting tab of contact module to be automatically added to the invoice line

When you click on your client who has the tax stamp, it will automatically add the invoice lines.

In the customer/supplier invoice, we find stamp tax only in the total amount.

In the accounting

If we change another customer who does not have a stamp tax, check the stamp tax line will be deleted
