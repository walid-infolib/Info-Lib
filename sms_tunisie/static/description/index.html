TUNISIE SMS

Send your SMS in Tunisia

To Configure:


Nothing to configure, just install the module.

Create your iap account under Settings > Technical > IAP > IAP Accounts, which is received from Tunisie SMS.

Set your country as "Tunisie" and click on the SMS icon.

Choose your Provider as Tunisie SMS and write your message.

In your phone you will receive the message from the sender you choosed.
